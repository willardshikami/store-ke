Gajimare Soaps

We believe in using traditional techniques in the creation of our 100% natural products. We source our unrefined Shea butter from Nigeria, our Cocoa Butter from Ghana, our Cold pressed Coconut oil from the coasts of Kenya and Sao Tome. Our essential oils are also sourced from the continent : Ylang Ylnag from Madagascar, Argan from Morrocco, Moringa, Lavendar, Rose Oil, Leleshwa Oil and Qasil from Kenya.

We ensure that all our scents are essential oil scents and NOT fragrance oils. Essential oils are luxurious additions. Fragrance oils are much cheaper and can be extremely toxic.
